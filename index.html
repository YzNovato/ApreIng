What is an anime?

Anime is an animation caracterized by style used in the work

Light Novel

Light Novel is a Japanese romance type aimed at teens, characterized by light writing and short chapters

Manga´

Manga are stories in Japanese comics, generally published in black and white and characterized by reading from right to left

Manhwa

Manhwa is the term used for South Korean comics. The digital version, more popular, known as webtoons, shows vertical reading and colored pages

Anime

Anime is the term used to refer to Japanese animations, including series and movies, with different art styles and stories ranging from day-to-day life to fantastic adventures in other worlds

To create anime you don't need to follow a specific sequence

Trailer

The main function of a trailer is to promote the anime, presenting some characters, scenes and a small preview of what may happen in the story.

Anime Opening

The opening creates the anime visual identity and shows characters and central themes, using scenes and symbolism, that works almost like small teasers of what is to come.

Anime Ending

The ending, like the opening, also contributes to the anime visual identity, but with a calmer tone, especially in the music.

Animation Techniques

Off-Screen Dialogue

This occurs when you cannot see the character's mouth moving while they are talking. Sometimes, you cannot even see the character.

Bank Animation

This technique is used for ready-made sequences repeated across multiple episodes.

  1. Special attacks
  2. Transformations
  3. Iconic moves

Animation Effects

Effects are used to hide or simplify some scenes.

  1. Smoke
  2. Fire
  3. Light/Shine
  4. Dust
