What is an anime?

The anime is an animation caracterized by style used in the work


Light Novel

Light Novel is one romance type japanese turned from teen, caractrized by one light writing and shorts chapters

Mangá

Mangás are stories in japanese comics, generally published in black and white and characterized by reading from right to left, both on the pages and in the comics

Manhwa

Manhwa is the term we used to south korean comics. The digital version more popular, known for webtoons, show vertical reading and colored pages

Anime

Anime is teh term se by japanese animations inclouding, series and movies, with diferents art styles and stories ranging from day a day life to fantastic adventures in other wolds

To create animes you don't need follow specific sequence

Trailer

The principal trailer function is promote the anime, pointing some characters, scenes and a little about the story may occur in the anime

Anime Opening

The opening create the anime visual identity and show characters and central themes, using scenes e symbolism that works almost like small teasers of what is to come

Anime Ending

The ending, like that the opening, also contributes to visual identity, but with a calmer tone, especially in song
